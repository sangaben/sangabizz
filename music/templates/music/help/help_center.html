üéµ Uploading Music

How to Upload Your Music:

  1. Go to your Artist Dashboard
  2. Click on "Upload Music"
  3. Fill in the song details (title, genre, etc.)
  4. Select your audio file (MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A)
  5. Add a cover image (optional but recommended)
  6. Click "Upload" and wait for approval

File Requirements:

  • Audio Formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A
  • Max File Size: 10MB
  • Cover Image: JPG, PNG (recommended: 1000x1000px)
  • Audio Quality: Minimum 128kbps, recommended 320kbps

Approval Process:

All uploaded songs go through a review process to ensure quality and copyright compliance. This usually takes 24-48 hours.
